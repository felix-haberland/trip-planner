# Travel Profile

## Who We Are

We are a fit couple from Munich, Germany. We love nature, hiking, and outdoor activities. We enjoy both cultural city experiences and adventure activities like safari, volcano climbs, and cloud forest exploration.

## Preferences

- **Temperature**: We prefer comfortable temperatures between 20-28°C during the day. We tolerate heat up to ~32°C but avoid extreme heat (>36°C) and cold (<15°C).
- **Activities**: Nature and hiking are our top priorities. We also enjoy golf, beach time, and exploring local culture and food.
- **Flight time**: Flight time should be proportional to trip length. As a rule of thumb:
  - **7-day trips**: Strongly prefer under 6 hours. Up to ~10h only if it's an exceptional fit.
  - **10-14 day trips**: Up to 10-12 hours is fine. Long-haul (12h+) only if the destination is a truly outstanding match for the trip's focus.
  - Destinations over 12 hours should almost never be suggested for trips under 10 days.
- **Accommodation**: We value good hotel quality and prefer mid-range to upscale options.
- **Safety**: Safety is a hard constraint — we avoid destinations with significant safety concerns.

## Travel Style

- We tolerate crowds and tourist areas but prefer off-the-beaten-path experiences when possible.
- We enjoy diverse experiences — mixing relaxation with adventure.
- Budget is considered but not the primary factor in our decisions.
- We typically travel for 7-14 days per trip.

## Special Interests

- Golf: Felix enjoys golf and we sometimes plan trips around good golf destinations.
- Hiking: We seek quality trails and scenic nature walks.
- Food & wine: We appreciate local cuisine and wine regions.
